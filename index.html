Aqua Sentinel is an innovative water quality monitoring initiative developed by the Innovators of Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology, Patiala. Our vision is to revolutionize environmental monitoring by creating a smart, affordable, and sustainable system that continously analyzes freshwater quality in real time.

The project combines IoT-enabled sensors, Spectroscopic data collection, and intelligent analytics to track vital parameters such as pH, temperature , turbidity ,dissolved oxygen and even presence of harmful metals.

Aqua Sentinel empowers communities, industries and government bodies to take proactive actions towards cleaner water and a healthier environment. It's not just a project but a mission to safeguard every drop for generations to come.
