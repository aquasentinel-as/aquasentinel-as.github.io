pH Level

Ideal Range: 6.5-8.5

pH measures how acidic or basic water is. Maintaining a neutral range is crucial for aquatic life and prevents pipe corrosion.

Aqua Sentinel continuously tracks pH to alert users when water becomes too acidic or alkaline.

Turbidity

Ideal Range: < 5 NTU

Turbidity refers to how clear or cloudy the water appears. High turbidity suggests high pollution, sediments, or microbial presence.

Aqua Sentinel detects turbidity to identify contamination early.

Ideal Range: < 500ppm

TDS measures minerals and salts dissolved in water. Higher TDS affects taste and may indicate contamination
